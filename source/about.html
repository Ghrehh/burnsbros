Who Are We?

Hello, we’re Burns Bros. & Co. We’re a newly formed (mostly digital) independent UX design studio that’s working remotely out of Belfast and South Yorkshire.

Our cross-functional creative team of designers, developers and strategists work with clients from across the UK & Ireland, the rest of Europe and beyond to build meaningful products that engage users and achieve goals.

We truly believe that well designed services and products can have a positive impact on the lives of real people. We ship work that helps passionate people achieve great things.

We’re a small but dedicated studio with over two decades worth of combined experience working in the realms of digital design, development and user research. We’re always on the lookout for one more great client. Let’s create something great together!

Why We Do What We Do?

In today’s regularly shifting digital landscape user uptake, retention and conversion relies heavily on a well designed experience. We love using well designed services and products, and we really love creating them. We get a hit from forging relationships with passionate people and helping them turn their ideas into reality.
